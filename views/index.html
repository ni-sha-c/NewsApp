The Literary Section does IIT Roorkee proud! IITR was ranked 4th overall in Nihilant, the yearly quizzing fest purely for the IITs and IIMs! IIMA, IIML and IITK were ranked first, second and third respectively. Haaris Matein (IV Chem), Vishal Battula (IV EE) and Abhinav Malhotra (IV Chem) fended off tough competition from all the teams to win the Biz Quiz. In addition to this, IITR was the only team other than IIML and IIMA to qualify for the final round of all quizzes!

The first edition of Nav Umang turned out to be great success, with participation from all hostels from both campuses! The overall rankings are as follows: 1. Malviya Bhawan 2. Cautley Bhawan 3. Jawahar Bhawan
